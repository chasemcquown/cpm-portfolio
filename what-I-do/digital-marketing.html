Digital Marketing - My Experience


I dove into the world of digital marketing in the fall of 2021, shortly after receiving my certificate of completion from UT Austin’s full stack coding bootcamp. I had some very basic knowledge of funnels, search engine optimization (SEO), and social media marketing, but it wasn’t very deep.


I enrolled in UT Austin’s digital marketing bootcamp and within the six months I spent learning, my digital marketing knowledge went parabolic. It felt like we covered EVERYTHING, from Google Analytics, to paid search ads, to competitor analysis on Semrush, to the nitty gritty world of search engine optimization (SEO), and more.


These newly learned digital marketing skills and techniques coupled with my growing knowledge of web development continued to round my edges and add new tools to my tool belt.
