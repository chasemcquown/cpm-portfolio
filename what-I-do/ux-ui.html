UX/UI Design - My Journey


My first introduction to UX/UI design was in April of 2021, at the time of my enrollment in UT Austin's coding bootcamp. I hadn't ever heard of UX before, but I did know that UI stood for user interface-and that was the depth of my knowledge. I didn't understand the importance of proper UX/UI design, and didn't care to at the time being that I was overwhelmed with my introduction to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.


Fast forward a year and the importance of UX/UI design became clear. It was during my enrollment in UT Austin’s digital marketing bootcamp that solid and thorough UX/ UI design was important for an array of different reasons. The experience a user has on your website affects the bounce rate of your site, your conversion rate(s), and even how well you rank in organic search results over time.


After realizing the impact UX/UI design has on all digital products, I found it necessary to enroll into UT Austin’s UX/UI bootcamp to further my knowledge of all things UX/UI related.
