UX/UI Design - My Experience


My first introduction to UX/UI design was in April of 2021 at the time of my enrollment in UT Austin's coding bootcamp. I had never heard of UX before, but I did know that UI stood for user interface... which was the full extent of my knowledge. I didn't understand the importance of proper UX/UI design, nor did I care at the time, being that I was overwhelmed with my introduction to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.


Fast forward a year and the importance of UX/UI design became clear during my enrollment in UT Austin’s digital marketing bootcamp. The experience a user has on your website affects the bounce rate of your site, your conversion rate(s), and even how well you rank in organic search results over time.


After realizing the impact UX/UI design has on all products (both physical goods and softwares), I found it necessary to enroll into UT Austin’s UX/UI bootcamp to add depth to my knowledge.
